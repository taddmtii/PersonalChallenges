Inventory Manager

You are building a simple inventory system for a small shop. Implement a class Inventory in C# that supports the following operations:

1. Add a product by name and quanitity. If product already exists, increase its quantity.

2. Remove a quantity of a product. If the quantity drops to zero or below remove the product entirely.

3. Get stock by returing the current quantity of a given product.

4. Track product that is most stocked, and alert the user with a list of all product names where quantity is below a given threshold.
